Methodology / Description

This type of research applies Jobs-to-be-Done and modern product discovery practice to compare customer segments based on behavioral evidence, triggers, and willingness to pay.

Comparison Attributes

Company / Context

Company type, size, stage, industry, and operating model.

Buyer Role

Decision-maker role and daily end-user role(s).

Core Pain

Specific behavioral problem this segment experiences.

Current Workarounds

What they do today instead of buying your product.

Decision Trigger

What event/threshold moves them to active buying.

Willingness to Pay

Evidence-based pricing range, model preference, and procurement friction.

Acquisition Channels

Where this persona is most reachable with high intent.

Editorial Priority

Directional wedge recommendation and rationale.
